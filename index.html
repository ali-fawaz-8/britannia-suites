Our Story

Your Home in Beirut

Located in the prestigious Raouche district — just a 3-minute walk from the iconic Raouche Rocks and the Beirut Corniche — Britannia Suites is a quiet sanctuary from the energy of the city. Step inside and the pace changes. The warmth of walnut wood, fresh flowers at the front desk, and a team that remembers your name make it feel less like a hotel and more like coming home.

Our fully furnished suites are designed for guests who want space, privacy, and comfort. Each one comes with a full kitchen, a private balcony, your own living room — everything you need to settle in and exhale. Whether you're here for a weekend or an extended stay, you'll find a calm, comfortable refuge that's entirely yours.
